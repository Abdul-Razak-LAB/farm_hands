## Recommended Reading Order

1. PRD — understand product scope and what will be built
2. BRD — align on why it matters to business outcomes
3. SRS — define exactly how software should behave and be tested

---

## Ownership and Update Guidance

- **PRD owner:** Product management
- **BRD owner:** Business/operations leadership
- **SRS owner:** Engineering + QA leadership

When adding or changing major features (for example marketplace, alerts channels, or offline architecture):
- Update PRD scope + acceptance criteria
- Update BRD business impact + KPI expectations
- Update SRS technical behavior + interface/validation rules
